Description

Insulation foam is a material used to reduce heat transfer and improve energy efficiency in buildings. It's typically made from polyurethane or polystyrene and comes in various forms, such as spray foam, rigid foam boards, and foam panels. Insulation foam is applied to walls, roofs, and floors to create a barrier that helps maintain indoor temperatures, reduce energy costs, and enhance comfort. Its excellent insulating properties make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial construction projects.
