Description

A cutting disc is a circular blade used with power tools like grinders or saws to cut through various materials such as metal, stone, or plastic. These discs are designed to spin at high speeds, allowing them to make precise and efficient cuts. Made from different materials depending on their intended use, cutting discs are essential in construction, metalworking, and DIY projects. They come in various sizes and types, each suited for specific tasks, ensuring that users can achieve clean and accurate cuts on their chosen material.
