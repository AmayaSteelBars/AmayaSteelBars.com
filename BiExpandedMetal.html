Description

Biexpanded metal, also known as double expanded metal, refers to a type of metal mesh that has been processed twice through an expanding machine. The process involves cutting and stretching the metal to create a mesh pattern with larger, more open spaces than standard expanded metal. This type of mesh is often used in applications where enhanced airflow, visibility, or drainage is required, while still providing a sturdy and durable structure. Biexpanded metal is commonly used in construction, filtration, and architectural applications.
