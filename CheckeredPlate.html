Description

A checkered plate is a type of metal sheet that has a pattern of raised shapes, like diamonds or lines, on one side. This textured surface helps prevent slipping, making it ideal for places where safety and traction are important, such as stairs, walkways, and industrial floors. It’s also used in vehicles and equipment to provide a sturdy, non-slip surface.
