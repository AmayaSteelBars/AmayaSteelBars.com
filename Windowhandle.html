Description

A window handle is a component attached to a window that allows you to open, close, and secure it. These handles come in various designs and mechanisms, such as lever handles, crank handles, and locking handles, each suited for different types of windows like casement, sliding, or awning windows. They are essential for the functionality and security of windows, providing ease of use and often incorporating locking mechanisms to ensure the window stays securely closed when needed.
