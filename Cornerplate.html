Description

A corner plate is a metal bracket used to connect and reinforce the corners of structures made with slotted angle bars. These L-shaped plates have multiple holes that align with the slots in the angle bars, allowing them to be securely fastened with bolts and nuts. By providing additional support at the joints, corner plates help ensure the stability and rigidity of the overall structure, making them essential for building sturdy shelving units, storage racks, and other frameworks. They also make assembly and disassembly easier, as the connections can be quickly tightened or loosened as needed.
