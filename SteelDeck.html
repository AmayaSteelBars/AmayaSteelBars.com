Description

A steel deck is a construction material made from corrugated steel sheets. These sheets are strong and lightweight, and they’re used to support concrete floors or roofs. The wavy, corrugated design makes the steel even stronger, allowing it to hold up heavy loads with less material. Steel decks are popular in commercial buildings and multi-story structures because they provide a solid base for concrete and help speed up construction. They are also durable and fire-resistant.
