Description

A grinding disc is a circular abrasive tool used with grinders to smooth, shape, or remove material from surfaces. These discs are made from abrasive grains bonded together, and they come in various sizes and types depending on the material they are designed to work on, such as metal, stone, or concrete. When attached to a grinder, the disc spins at high speeds, allowing it to grind down surfaces efficiently.
