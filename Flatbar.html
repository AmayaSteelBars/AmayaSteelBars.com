Description

A flatbar refers to a type of steel bar that is flat in shape, often used in construction, manufacturing, and various structural applications. It comes in various widths and thicknesses and is known for its strength and versatility. Flatbars can be used in framing, braces, supports, base plates, and more. They are commonly made from different grades of steel to suit specific needs and can be easily cut, welded, and formed.
