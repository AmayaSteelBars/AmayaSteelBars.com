Roofing Nail

Description

A roofing nail, particularly one with an "umbrella-like" shape, has a large, rounded head that resembles an umbrella. This design offers excellent coverage and helps hold down roofing materials more effectively, preventing them from being lifted or damaged by wind. The umbrella shape provides extra stability and minimizes the risk of tearing around the nail hole, making it ideal for materials like asphalt shingles.
