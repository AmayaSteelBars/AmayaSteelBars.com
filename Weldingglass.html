Description

Welding glass is a type of protective eyewear designed to shield the eyes from the intense light and harmful ultraviolet (UV) and infrared (IR) radiation produced during welding. It typically features dark-tinted lenses that filter out these harmful rays, preventing eye damage such as welder's flash or arc eye.
