Description

The Longspan rib type, or Long Span Rib Roof, is a type of roofing made from galvanized steel. Its designed with a ribbed pattern that provides strength and durability. This roofing style is popular for covering large areas like warehouses and gymnasiums because it can span long distances without needing many supports. It’s also cost-effective since it uses fewer materials to cover a given area. Overall, its a strong, durable, and economical choice for roofing large buildings.
