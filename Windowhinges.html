Description

Window hinges are the hardware components that allow windows to open and close smoothly. They are typically attached to the window frame and the sash (the part of the window that moves). Depending on the type of window, there are different kinds of hinges, such as butt hinges, friction hinges, and pivot hinges. These hinges ensure that the window operates correctly, providing stability and ease of use.
