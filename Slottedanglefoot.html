Description

A Slotted Angle Foot for slotted angle bars is a base component designed to provide stability and protect the floor. These footings are typically made of durable plastic and fit onto the ends of the vertical slotted angle bars. They help distribute the weight of the structure evenly, preventing damage to the floor and adding stability to the overall framework. Plastic footings are easy to install and are often used in shelving units, storage racks, and other structures made with slotted angle bars to ensure they remain upright and secure.
